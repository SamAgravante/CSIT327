FAQs

Have questions? This is where you'll find answers about StonksTracker. Learn how to navigate the app, understand price updates, and make the most of your tracking experience.

What is a price deviation and how is it calculated?

A price deviation refers to the difference between the current price of an item and its historical average. It's calculated by comparing the item's latest price with its average price over a set period.

Why do prices fluctuate?

Prices can fluctuate due to various factors such as changes in supply and demand, market trends, and updates or new releases in the game.
